Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions that may help you while using the voting system.

1. How do I register as a voter?

Open the Register page, fill in your name, mobile number, email address, voter ID number, and date of birth, then submit the form to create your account.

2. What should I do if I do not receive the OTP?

Wait for a few seconds and use the resend OTP option. If the issue continues, contact support through phone or email for assistance.

3. Can I view candidates before voting?

Yes. The Candidates page displays candidate names, party details, and related information before you proceed with voting.

4. Is this a real election platform?

No. VoteIndia is a front-end academic project prototype created to demonstrate the structure and design of an online voting system.
